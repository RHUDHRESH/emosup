# Initial Concept
An empathetic AI-powered emotional support companion that leverages multi-agent intelligence (CrewAI) and local LLMs (Ollama) to provide 24/7 support, mood tracking, and coping strategies through both a modern web interface and a data-focused application.

# Product Vision
The Emotional Support Companion is designed as a versatile tool for individuals seeking emotional support, students of AI empathy, and mental health enthusiasts. Its primary goal is to provide accessible, empathetic companionship while helping users track their emotional patterns and demonstrating the efficacy of multi-agent systems in mental health contexts.

# Core Value Propositions
- **Intelligent Empathy:** Real-time emotion detection and sentiment analysis powered by specialized agents.
- **Data-Driven Self-Care:** Visual mood dashboards and personalized coping suggestions derived from conversation history.
- **Collaborative AI:** A multi-agent orchestration where an "Empathy Analyst" and "Compassionate Companion" work together to deliver validated, supportive responses.

# User Experience & Interfaces
The product offers a dual-interface experience to cater to diverse user needs:
- **Consumer Web Experience:** A polished Next.js frontend for a seamless, modern interaction.
- **Operator/Researcher View:** A Streamlit application optimized for rapid analysis and functional simplicity.
